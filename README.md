## About APT Ghana

**Automation & Plant Technologies Limited (APT Ghana)** is a leading industrial automation and engineering solutions company headquartered in Accra, Ghana. Founded in **2009**, APT has grown into one of the most trusted distributors and technical partners for manufacturing, mining, quarrying, and construction industries across Ghana and the broader West African region.

The company is officially certified as a **General Distributor for Schneider Electric** in Ghana — the first of its kind to establish a Schneider Electric Innovation Center on the African continent. With over **15 years of experience** in electrical engineering and automation, APT Ghana combines global-standard products with deep local expertise to deliver reliable, high-performance industrial solutions.

APT's founders have remained actively involved in growing the business, building strategic partnerships with world-class international suppliers, investing in staff development, and continuously expanding the company's product and service offering.

## The Schneider Electric Innovation Center

On **16 June 2023**, APT Ghana inaugurated the **Schneider Electric Innovation Center** at its premises in the North Industrial Area, Accra — the **first of its kind on the African continent**.

The event was attended by over 100 guests including Mr. Ajibola Akindele MFR, Country President of Schneider Electric West Africa, along with clients, suppliers, partners, and industry stakeholders.

### Features of the Innovation Center

- **Conference Room** — Dedicated space for industry meetings, presentations, and knowledge-sharing events
- **Training Center** — A structured learning environment for engineers and technicians to gain hands-on knowledge of Schneider Electric products and systems
- **Product Showroom** — An open-area display of Schneider Electric's full portfolio, from energy distribution to industrial automation and sustainable technologies

### Purpose

The center serves as a hub for **energy efficiency, digital transition, and sustainability** in Ghana and West Africa. It is designed to empower local engineers and technicians with the skills needed to drive sustainable energy solutions, support net-zero ambitions, and participate in Africa's industrial digital transformation.

> *"The vision of the innovation hub was born out of a drive to emphasise Africa's critical role in the global push for digital innovations in sustainable energy solutions towards achieving net-zero emissions."*
> — **Elie Malak**, Co-founder & Director, APT Ghana
